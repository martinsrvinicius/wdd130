History

Our journey began in the rugged wilderness of the Pacific Northwest, where a group of adventure enthusiasts shared a passion for navigating challenging river rapids. Founded in 1995 by experienced river guides Mark Thompson and Sarah Rodriguez, our water rafting company emerged from a deep love for outdoor exploration and a commitment to providing safe, exhilarating river experiences.

Starting with just two rafts and a dream, we gradually built our reputation by offering guided tours through some of the most spectacular river canyons in the region. Our early expeditions focused on the challenging rapids of the McKenzie and Deschutes Rivers, where we developed a unique approach that balanced adventure with safety.
